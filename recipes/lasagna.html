LASAGNA

DESCRIPTION

Instructions Make the Meat Sauce Heat a large pot or Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Cook the ground beef and sausage until browned, breaking it up. Drain excess fat if needed. Add onion and garlic; sauté 3–5 minutes until softened. Stir in tomato paste, crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, water/wine, sugar, and seasonings. Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat and cook uncovered for 30–45 minutes, stirring occasionally. Taste and adjust seasoning. The sauce should be thick and flavorful.
Prepare the Ricotta Filling In a bowl, mix ricotta, egg, Parmesan, parsley, salt, and pepper until smooth. Set aside. Cook the Noodles (if not no-boil) Boil lasagna noodles in salted water according to package directions until al dente. Drain and rinse with cold water. Lay flat on a sheet to prevent sticking. (No-boil noodles work great too—just add a bit more sauce or water.) Assemble the Lasagna Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom. Layer 3–4 noodles. Spread ⅓ of the ricotta mixture. Sprinkle with mozzarella. Add a generous layer of meat sauce. Repeat 2–3 more times, ending with noodles, sauce, mozzarella, and extra Parmesan on top.

INGREDIENTS

Meat Sauce

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef
  • ½ lb (225g) Italian sausage (sweet or hot, casings removed)
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3–4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes or whole San Marzano tomatoes (puréed)
  • 1 (15 oz) can tomato sauce
  • 2 (6 oz) cans tomato paste
  • ½ cup water or red wine
  • 2 tbsp sugar (balances acidity)
  • 2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • ½ tsp fennel seeds (optional, crushed)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Optional: sliced mushrooms or red pepper flakes for heat
  • Cheese Filling
  • 15–16 oz (425–450g) ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley (plus extra for garnish)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Other
  • 12–16 lasagna noodles (regular or no-boil)
  • 3–4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Extra Parmesan for topping

STEPS

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large pot, cook ground beef and sausage over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add diced onion and cook until softened, then add garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, water or wine, sugar, Italian seasoning, basil, fennel seeds, salt, and pepper.
  5. Simmer sauce on low heat for 20–30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, Parmesan, parsley, salt, and pepper until smooth.
  7. If using regular noodles, boil according to package instructions and drain.
  8. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a baking dish.
  9. Layer noodles over sauce, then spread ricotta mixture, add mozzarella, and top with more sauce.
  10. Repeat layers (noodles, ricotta, mozzarella, sauce) until ingredients are used, finishing with sauce and mozzarella on top.
  11. Sprinkle extra Parmesan on top.
  12. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  13. Remove foil and bake an additional 20–25 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  14. Let lasagna rest for 10–15 minutes before slicing and serving.
