DESCRIPTION

Instructions Boil noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside. Heat vegetable oil in a pan or wok over medium-high heat. Add garlic and onion, stir-fry until fragrant. Add mixed vegetables and cook for 2–3 minutes until slightly tender. If using egg, push vegetables to one side, scramble egg, then mix it in. Add cooked protein (if using) and stir well. Add the cooked noodles to the pan and toss everything together. Pour in soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, black pepper, and salt. Stir-fry for another 2–3 minutes until well combined and heated through. Serve hot.

INGREDIENTS

Meat Sauce

  • Ingredients
  • 200–250g noodles (egg noodles or spaghetti)
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, cabbage, green beans)
  • 1 egg (optional)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce (optional)
  • 1 tsp sesame oil (optional)
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: cooked chicken, beef, shrimp, or tofu

STEPS

  1. Boil noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Add garlic and onion, stir-fry until fragrant.
  4. Add mixed vegetables and cook for 2–3 minutes until slightly tender.
  5. If using egg, push vegetables to one side, scramble egg, then mix it in.
  6. Add cooked protein (if using) and stir well.
  7. Add the cooked noodles to the pan and toss everything together.
  8. Pour in soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, black pepper, and salt.
  9. Stir-fry for another 2–3 minutes until well combined and heated through.
  10. Serve hot.
